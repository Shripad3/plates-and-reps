Privacy Policy

Plates & Reps · Last updated June 2026

Plates & Reps collects account information, nutrition and workout logs, body metrics, social activity, and AI chat content to provide tracking and coaching features.

Data we collect

  • Email address, display name, and profile settings
  • Food and workout logs, weight entries, and streaks
  • Photos and voice recordings you submit for AI food logging
  • Subscription status via Apple and RevenueCat

How we use data

To operate the app, sync your account, provide AI coach responses, show social activity to your connections, and manage subscriptions. Food photos and voice recordings are processed using Groq.

Third parties

Supabase (database and auth), Groq (AI), RevenueCat and Apple (purchases), and optional Sentry (crash reporting in production).

Account deletion

Delete your account in the app: Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile and logs. Cancel active subscriptions in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
