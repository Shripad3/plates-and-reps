Privacy Policy

Plates & Reps · Last updated July 2026

Plates & Reps ("we", "us") collects account information, nutrition and workout logs, body metrics, social activity, and AI chat content to provide tracking and coaching features.

Data we collect

  • Email address, display name, and profile settings
  • Food and workout logs, and weight entries
  • Photos and voice recordings you submit for AI food logging
  • Purchase status via Apple and RevenueCat
  • Usage data needed to enforce free-tier limits

How we use data

To operate the app, sync your account across devices, provide Plates & Reps AI responses, show social feed activity to people you connect with, and manage subscriptions.

AI coach & Groq

Plates & Reps AI is powered by Groq, Inc. When you use the coach, we send Groq the information needed to answer you: your display name, height, activity level, goals and target macros, your nutrition totals and logged foods, your workout and cardio history, your logged body weight, and the messages you type. Food photos and voice recordings you submit for AI food logging are also processed through Groq. We ask for your consent before the coach is used for the first time, and you can decline. Groq processes this data to generate a response and does not use it to train its models.

Third parties

We share data with the following services only as needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Groq, Inc. — AI coach and food-photo/voice analysis
  • Google — only if you choose Sign in with Google
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and in-app purchases
  • Open Food Facts — food searches and scanned barcodes are sent to look up nutrition data
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Sentry — optional crash reporting in production builds

We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Children's privacy

Plates & Reps is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us so we can delete it.

Account deletion

You can delete your account in the app under Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile and associated logs. Active subscriptions must be cancelled in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
